Welcome to Capture The NEAT!

Using this program, you can get your hands dirty with NEAT, or Neuro Evolotion with Augmenting Topologies. Using NEAT, each 'bot' will evolve over time, slowly learning how to better play the game of Capture The Flag. Each bot is its own species, and will not 'mate' with bots from other species. By adjusting the score point values, hidden layers, etc., you can drastically change how each bot evolves.
